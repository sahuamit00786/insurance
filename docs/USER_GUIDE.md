## 1. Logging In

Open the system website. Enter your **email address** and **password**, then click **Login**.

- Your login details are given to you by your administrator.
- If you cannot log in or forgot your password, contact your administrator.
- You will be logged out automatically after 8 hours of inactivity.

---

## 2. Dashboard — Your Home Screen

After logging in, you land on the **Dashboard**. This screen gives you a quick summary of the business at a glance.

### Summary Cards

At the top you will see four numbers:

- **Total Clients** — How many clients are in the system
- **Active Insurance** — How many policies are currently active
- **Total Documents** — How many files have been uploaded
- **Total Staff** — How many team members are using the system

### Upcoming Birthdays

Shows clients whose birthday is coming up in the next 7 days. A great reminder to send wishes or make a personal call — which helps build client relationships.

### Expiring Soon

Shows policies whose next premium payment is due within the next 7 days. Follow up with these clients so they do not miss a payment and risk their policy lapsing.

### Maturing Soon

Shows policies that will reach their maturity date within the next 30 days. These clients may want to renew or discuss their next plan.

## 3. Clients — Managing Your Clients

**Click "Clients" in the left menu.**

This is where all your client records are stored. Everything else in the system — policies, payments, documents — is linked to a client.

---

### Viewing Your Client List

You will see a table with all clients. You can:

- **Search** by typing a name, phone number, IC number, or email in the search bar
- **Click any client row** to open their full profile

---

### Adding a New Client

1. Click the **Add Client** button (top right corner)
2. Fill in the client's details:
   - **Full Name** — required
   - **IC / Identification Number** — required
   - **Date of Birth** — important for birthday reminders
   - **Phone Number** — for calls and WhatsApp
   - **Email Address** — for sending messages
   - **Address** — optional
3. Click **Save**

The client is now in the system and ready to have policies, payments, and documents added.

---

### Opening a Client Profile

Click on any client's name in the list. Their profile will open with the following sections:

| Tab | What you will find there |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | Basic information — name, IC, date of birth, phone, email, address |
| **Insurances** | All policies belonging to this client |
| **Payments** | All payments recorded for this client |
| **Documents** | All files uploaded for this client |
| **Notes** | Internal notes written about this client |

---

### Editing a Client

Open the client profile and click **Edit**. Change the details and click **Save**.

---

### Deleting a Client

Click the delete icon next to the client. This will permanently remove the client and all their records. Only do this if you are absolutely sure — this cannot be undone.

> Only staff with delete permission can do this.

---

## 4. Insurances — Managing Policies

Policies can be viewed in two ways:

- **From the Insurances menu** — see all policies across all clients
- **From inside a client profile** — see only that client's policies

---

### The Insurances Menu (All Policies)

Click **Insurances** in the left menu to see every policy in the system. Use the filters at the top to narrow down what you see:

- Search by client name or policy number
- Filter by plan type, status, payment mode, or coverage provider
- Filter by date range
- Show only expired policies or only active ones

This is useful for bulk follow-ups — for example, finding all policies expiring this month.

---

### Adding a Policy to a Client

1. Open the client's profile
2. Click the **Insurances** tab
3. Click **Add Insurance**
4. Fill in the details:
   - **Policy Number** — required
   - **Plan** — what type of plan (e.g. life, medical, investment)
   - **Status** — Active, Lapsed, Surrendered, etc.
   - **Issued Date** — when the policy started
   - **Maturity Date** — when the policy ends
   - **Premium Amount** — how much the client pays
   - **Premium Due Date** — when the next payment is due
   - **Payment Mode** — monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, yearly
   - **Coverage Provider** — the insurance company
   - **Buying For** — self, spouse, child, parents, etc.
   - **Note** — any extra remarks
5. Click **Save**

---

### Editing or Removing a Policy

On any policy row, use the **edit (pencil)** or **delete (bin)** icons.

---

## 5. Appointments — Scheduling Meetings

**Click "Appointments" in the left menu.**

Use this to track client meetings, calls, or follow-up sessions.

---

### Viewing Appointments

You will see a **calendar view**. Click on any date to see what appointments are scheduled.

---

### Adding an Appointment

1. Click **Add Appointment** or click directly on a date in the calendar
2. Fill in:
   - **Client** — select a client from the list, OR
   - **Guest Name** — type a name if the person is not yet in the system
   - **Date** — required
   - **Time** — optional
   - **Appointment Type** — e.g. meeting, follow-up, renewal discussion
   - **Note** — what the meeting is about
3. Click **Save**

---

### Editing or Cancelling

Use the **edit** or **delete** icons on any appointment.

---

## 6. Documents — Storing Client Files

**Access: Open a client profile → Documents tab**

Store important files for each client — IC copies, policy PDFs, claim forms, health declarations, and more.

---

### Uploading a File

1. Open the client profile
2. Click the **Documents** tab
3. Click **Upload Document**
4. Choose the **Document Type** (e.g. IC Copy, Policy Document, Claim Form)
5. Select the file from your computer (PDF or image files, up to 10 MB)
6. Click **Upload**

---

### Viewing a File

Click the **View** icon next to any document. It will open in a new browser tab.

---

### Removing a File

Click the **Delete** icon next to the document. Only staff with delete permission can do this.

---

## 7. Payments — Recording Payments

**Access: Open a client profile → Payments tab**

Keep a record of every premium payment a client makes.

---

### Recording a Payment

1. Open the client profile
2. Click the **Payments** tab
3. Click **Add Payment**
4. Fill in:
   - **Payment Date** — when the payment was made
   - **Amount (RM)** — how much was paid
   - **Receipt Number** — optional, for reference
   - **Payment Mode** — cash, bank transfer, cheque, etc.
   - **Payment Method** — how it was done
   - **Linked Policy** — which insurance policy this payment is for
   - **Remarks** — any extra notes
5. Click **Save**

---

### Editing or Removing a Payment

Use the **edit** or **delete** icons on any payment record.

---

## 8. Notes — Internal Reminders

**Access: Open a client profile → Notes tab**

Write private notes about a client. These are internal — clients cannot see them. Use notes to remember follow-up actions, record call summaries, or flag special situations.

---

### Writing a Note

1. Open the client profile
2. Click the **Notes** tab
3. Click **Add Note**
4. Fill in:
   - **Title** — a short label (optional, e.g. "Called client", "Renewal discussion")
   - **Note** — what you want to record
   - **Color** — pick a color to help you organize (e.g. red for urgent, green for done)
   - **Pin** — turn this on to keep important notes at the top of the list
5. Click **Save**

---

### Editing or Deleting a Note

Use the **edit** or **delete** icons on each note card.

---

## 9. Templates — Message Templates

**Click "Templates" in the left menu.**

Create ready-made message templates that you can use for WhatsApp, SMS, or email. The system automatically fills in the client's details when you preview a message.

---

### Creating a Template

1. Click **New Template**
2. Enter a **Name** — e.g. "Birthday Wish", "Renewal Reminder", "Payment Due Alert"
3. Write your message in the **Body** field
4. Use the available shortcodes to automatically insert client information (the system replaces them with real data when previewing)
5. Click **Save**

---

### Previewing a Template

1. Click **Preview** on any template
2. Select a client from the list
3. The system shows the message with that client's actual information filled in
4. Copy the message and send it via WhatsApp or any other channel

---

### Editing or Deleting a Template

Use the **edit** or **delete** icons on each template.

---

## 10. Reports — Exporting Data

**Click "Reports" in the left menu.**

Generate and download data from the system as a spreadsheet (Excel/CSV file). Useful for monthly reviews, audits, or sharing data with management.

---

### How to Generate a Report

1. Select the **Report Type**:
   - **Clients** — list of all clients with their insurance summary
   - **Insurances** — all policies with full details
   - **Payments** — full payment history
   - **Notes** — all client notes

2. Select the **columns** you want to include — tick only what you need

3. Apply **filters** if needed — narrow down by date range, client, plan type, status, etc.

4. Click **Generate**

5. Click **Export to CSV** to download the file and open it in Excel

---

## 11. Staff — Managing Your Team

**Click "Staff" in the left menu.**

> This section is only available to Admin and Superadmin users.

---

### Viewing Your Team

You will see a list of all staff members with their name, email, role, and status.

---

### Adding a New Staff Member

1. Click **Add Staff**
2. Fill in:
   - **Full Name** — required
   - **Email Address** — this is what they will use to log in
   - **Phone Number** — optional
   - **Password** — a secure password to give them on their first login
   - **Role** — see roles explained below
   - **Status** — Active (can log in) or Inactive (cannot log in)
3. Click **Create Member**

The new staff member can now log in using their email and the password you set.

---

### Roles Explained

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Superadmin** | Complete access to everything. This is the owner-level account. |
| **Admin** | Full access to all features including staff management. |
| **Manager** | Access set by the admin through permissions. |
| **Staff** | Access set by the admin through permissions. |

---

### Setting What a Staff Member Can Access

Each staff member (Manager or Staff role) only sees what you allow them to see.

1. Click the **Key icon** next to a staff member
2. For each section of the system, you can turn on or off:
   - **View** — can they see this section at all?
   - **Add** — can they add new records?
   - **Edit** — can they update existing records?
   - **Delete** — can they remove records?
3. Click **Save Permissions**

If a staff member has no permission for a section, that menu item will not appear for them when they log in.

**Example:** If you turn off all permissions for "Clients" for a staff member, they will not see the Clients menu at all.

---

### Resetting a Staff Member's Password

1. Click the **Edit icon** next to the staff member
2. Enter a new password in the **Password** field
3. Click **Save changes**

Give the new password to the staff member so they can log in.

---

### Deactivating a Staff Member

If someone leaves your company or should no longer have access:

1. Click the **Edit icon** next to them
2. Change **Status** to **Inactive**
3. Click **Save changes**

They will no longer be able to log in. You can reactivate them at any time by setting Status back to Active.

---

## 12. My Profile — Your Account

Click on your **name or photo** at the top right corner, then click **Profile**.

---

### What you can update

- **Full Name** — how your name appears in the system
- **Email Address** — your login email (must be unique)

---

### Changing Your Password

1. Scroll down to the **Change Password** section
2. Enter your **current password**
3. Enter your **new password** — you will see three green ticks appear as you meet each requirement
4. Re-enter the new password to confirm
5. Click **Save changes**

If you do not want to change your password, leave the password fields blank — your name and email will still be saved.

---

## Quick Tips

- **Always search before adding** a client — avoid creating duplicate records
- **Keep Premium Due Dates updated** — this is what drives the Expiring Soon alerts on the Dashboard
- **Use Notes** to record every client interaction — it helps the whole team stay informed
- **Use Templates** to save time on repetitive messages like birthday wishes and renewal reminders
- **Check the Dashboard daily** — it shows you who needs attention today
